Glitches (Eroding Cities)


I chose night city photos which had bright lights and the same predominant colors, red and blue, as my theme. I created the Glitch images by modifying the original images' text files with TextEdit. I deleted, copied, pasted, and cut sections of different sizes and rearranged them throughout the files. What I really like about the Glitch images is that the results were very different and they look like different techniques were used. The first glitch is mainly composed of large chunks of the original image arranged differently and with altered tones and colors. The second image is characterized by its pixelated quality and in certain sections some outlines of details from other parts of the image can be seen.
